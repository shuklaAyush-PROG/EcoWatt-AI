# EcoWatt AI - Sustainability Logic

---

# 1. Carbon Footprint Calculation

Formula:

CO₂ Emission (kg/month) =
Monthly Units × 0.82

Assumption:
1 kWh electricity ≈ 0.82 kg CO₂

---

# Example

Monthly Units = 650

650 × 0.82

= 533 kg CO₂/month

---

# 2. Annual Carbon Emission

Formula:

Annual CO₂ =
Monthly CO₂ × 12

Example:

533 × 12

= 6396 kg CO₂/year

---

# 3. Tree Equivalent Calculation

Approximation:

1 tree absorbs ≈ 21 kg CO₂/year

Formula:

Trees Needed =
Annual CO₂ / 21

Example:

6396 / 21

≈ 305 trees
